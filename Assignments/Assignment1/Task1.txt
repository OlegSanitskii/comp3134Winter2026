Task 1 – Company Information (Google)

Company Name: Google LLC
Industry: Technology / Internet Services
Parent Company: Alphabet Inc.

Number of Employees:
As of 2025 estimates, Alphabet Inc. (Google) employs approximately 190,000 employees worldwide.
Recent (Late 2025): Around 185,719 (March 2025) or 190,167 (Sept 2025).

Chief Executive Officer (CEO):
Sundar Pichai
CEO of Google LLC and Alphabet Inc.

Chief Financial Officer (CFO):
Anat Ashkenazi
CFO of Alphabet Inc. (parent company of Google).
She assumed this role on July 31, 2024, replacing former CFO Ruth Porat.

President:
Google does not currently have a separate President position.
Executive leadership responsibilities are handled by the CEO.
